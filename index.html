Roadmap

This roadmap outlines our goals and where we want to take Avatar Squad. We have a lot of ideas and concepts that we are working on.

1. Launch of CupLanders

5ETH giveaway to 10 CupLanders Holders when sold out.

2. Community Wallet

20% of the mint funds will be used to sweep the floor (buy the lowest CupLanders) to exclude all flippers. If the total amount is not necessary to sweep the floor, the difference will help to make some promotions and giveaways for CupLanders holders.

3. Reveal

24 Hours after the CupLanders mint is complete, the reveal will happen, and you will be able to see your CupLanders NFT for the first time. Shortly after that, you will also be able to see the rank of your CupLanders with tools like RaritySniper.

4. Charity

20 000$ to a charity organization chosen by the community.

5. Roadmap 2.0

Establishment of DAO system for CupLanders community. They will have majors vote to essentially orientate the continuation of the roadmap and the project. Keep in mind that this is only CupLanders roadmap 1.0. The next part of the roadmap will be released when the DAO system will be in place and the Cupers will have made vote to determine the things they want to see in priority on this project. Those who will hold multiple CupLanders will logically have multiple votes.
